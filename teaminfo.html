Experience the excitement of basketball in Karachi, Pakistan. LOB began five years ago as a non-profit organization and has remained a non-profit organization to this day, providing a strong platform for the widespread basketball talent in Pakistan. The league, owned by Shahzain Malik who is helped by his team, has come a long way. They have secured many sponsorships and now a contract with Geo Super, allowing LOB to gain the reach many thought they never could. LOB is going nationwide, and you're a part of the journey.
